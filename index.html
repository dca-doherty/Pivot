About Carol A. Taylor

Carol A. Taylor is a woman who has walked through the valley of loss and emerged with a powerful testimony of God's faithfulness. As a single mother who raised two college students while working demanding jobs in medical management and teaching, she understood hardship long before losing her beloved husband Jim.

For 15 years, Isaiah 61 was Carol's life chapter, though she didn't fully understand why until Jim's sudden passing on October 18th. Through her grief journey, God revealed this scripture as a blueprint for healing—not just for her, but for anyone facing loss.

Carol's story includes witnessing miraculous healings, experiencing God's supernatural provision, and learning to trust His promises even in the darkest valley. From seeing her friend Donna healed of psoriasis through prayer to experiencing God's double portion provision, Carol has lived the promises of Isaiah 61 in powerful ways.

Now she shares her journey to help others pivot from their own losses to abundant life, knowing that God makes beautiful things out of ashes.

The Journey from Loss to Life

Following the Blueprint of Isaiah 61

1. Anointed for Purpose

God chooses and equips us even in our brokenness. You are anointed to bring good news and bind up broken hearts, even when your own heart is shattered.

2. Year of the Lord's Favor

Declare victory over the enemy's lies. This is your year of grace, acceptance, and God's special favor. His promises are bigger than your pain.

3. Beauty for Ashes

Exchange mourning for gladness, receive the oil of joy, and put on the garment of praise. God is making you new and beautiful.

4. Rebuild and Restore

Rise from devastation to become the repairer of the breach, restoring what was lost for generations. Your story will bless others.
