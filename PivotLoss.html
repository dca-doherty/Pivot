About Carol A. Taylor

Carol A. Taylor is a woman who has walked through the valley of loss and emerged with a powerful testimony of God's faithfulness. As a single mother who raised two college students while working demanding jobs, she understood hardship long before losing her beloved husband Jim.

For 15 years, Isaiah 61 was Carol's life chapter, though she didn't fully understand why until Jim's sudden passing. Through her grief journey, God revealed this scripture as a blueprint for healing—not just for her, but for anyone facing loss.

Carol's story is one of miraculous provision, from witnessing healing prayers answered to experiencing God's double portion after loss. Now she shares her journey to help others pivot from their own losses to abundant life.
